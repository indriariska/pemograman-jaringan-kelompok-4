Frequently Asked

Common questions about my services

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in React, Next.js, Laravel, Node.js, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Solidity. I'm also proficient in Vue.js, Python, Docker, AWS, and various blockchain platforms. I choose the best tech stack for your specific needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Small websites take 4-8 weeks, full-featured web apps take 10-16 weeks, and enterprise solutions can take 3-6 months. I provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes! All projects include initial support and bug fixes. Professional and Enterprise plans include extended support periods. I also offer ongoing maintenance packages for long-term projects.

What is your development process?

I follow a 6-step process: Discovery & Planning → Design & Prototyping → Development → Testing & Refinement → Deployment & Launch → Support & Maintenance. You'll have regular updates and demos throughout.

Can you work with my existing team?

Absolutely! I can integrate with your existing team, work as a dedicated resource, or provide consulting. I'm experienced in remote collaboration and using standard development practices like Git and Agile methodologies.

How do you ensure code quality and security?

I follow industry best practices: code reviews, unit testing, integration testing, security audits, and performance optimization. For blockchain projects, I conduct thorough security audits. All code is documented and maintainable.
